## Managing Your Preferences

### Turn Off a Channel
1. Go to "Channels" tab
2. Click toggle to disable (it will turn gray)
3. Click "Save Settings"
4. You will no longer receive notifications on that channel

### Update Contact Information
1. Go to "Contact Info" tab
2. Change any field
3. Click "Save Settings"
4. You can test new contact with "Send Test"

### Adjust Quiet Hours
1. Go to "Preferences" tab
2. Toggle "Enable quiet hours"
3. Set start time (e.g., 22:00 for 10 PM)
4. Set end time (e.g., 08:00 for 8 AM)
5. Click "Save Settings"

**Note:** Notifications during quiet hours will be queued and sent after quiet hours end.

## Troubleshooting

### âŒ Not Receiving Email Notifications
**Check:**
1. Is email channel enabled? (Look for toggle in "Channels" tab)
2. Check your email spam/junk folder
3. Verify email address is correct
4. Click "Send Test" to verify setup

**Solution:**
- Make sure notifications are enabled in preferences
- Add jobintel@company.com to your contacts
- Check your email filters

### âŒ Not Receiving WhatsApp Messages
**Check:**
1. Is WhatsApp channel enabled?
2. Is WhatsApp number correct? (+91 format)
3. Is WhatsApp account active?

**Solution:**
- Update WhatsApp number in Contact Info
- Try a test message first
- Make sure WhatsApp is installed and active

### âŒ Not Receiving Telegram Messages
**Check:**
1. Is Telegram channel enabled?
2. Is Telegram ID correct? (Numeric ID)
3. Have you started the JobIntel bot?

**Solution:**
- Go to Telegram and find @JobIntelBot
- Click START to activate the bot
- Update your Telegram ID in settings

### âŒ Didn't Receive Test Notification
**Steps:**
1. Go to "Channels" tab
2. Make sure the channel is enabled (toggle is ON)
3. Click "Send Test"
4. Select the channel
5. Wait 10-15 seconds
6. Check your notification channel

If still not received:
- Verify all contact information is correct
- Try a different channel
- Contact support

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many notifications will I get?**  
A: Depends on your profile and settings. Average users get 2-5 notifications per day.

**Q: Can I get notifications for all jobs?**  
A: Only jobs with 60%+ match score are sent (you control threshold in settings).

**Q: What are quiet hours for?**  
A: To avoid notifications during sleep/work hours. They'll be queued and sent after.

**Q: Can I unsubscribe from all notifications?**  
A: Yes, click the unsubscribe link in any email notification.

**Q: How do I get my Telegram ID?**  
A: Message @userinfobot on Telegram and it will show your ID.

**Q: Are my contact details secure?**  
A: Yes, all data is encrypted and stored securely.

**Q: Can I use the same number for WhatsApp and phone?**  
A: Yes, you can. The system will send via WhatsApp if enabled.
